## Claim Rule

Avoid first-in-history claims. The defensible claim is that each item is a named, transparent, decision-ready composite built from established Sentinel-2 spectral mechanisms, domain gates, and confounder rejection.

## Authorship Rule

Authorship means the names, recipes, gate logic, public-good framing, bookmark curation, ranking, and app implementation. It does not mean ownership of Sentinel-2 bands, common indices, established fields, or verified detection.

Strongest current authorship claims:

- CSRC - most ownable public-health scum-risk workflow for small waters.
- TRSI - most ownable tailings river-shock emergency-screening workflow.
- LFGVI - most ownable landfill boundary-ring vegetation intrusion workflow.
- SWRI - most ownable sewage-water release screening workflow.
- DWCI - strong civic source-water injury decision product.
- RRFI - strong ecological refuge-failure framing.
- EPDI - strong source-to-delivery sediment pulse workflow.
- HSAI - strong honest shelter-absence vulnerability score, with no thermal claim.

More crowded prior-art areas need tighter claims: EC-ACI, SF-EII, WDA-CSI, CD-UAI, PETI, and TDR-ASI should be framed as applied decision workflows rather than novel spectral science.

## Limn Produced-Water Rule

PWCI, ASAI, OBEC, LBI, VSI, and BPI are imported as Limn-derived produced-water calibrations. Limn's novelty review removed exclusive authorship claims for these indices, so Civic Sentinel should describe them as meaningful Globe & Atlas / Limn workflows integrated into this broader atlas.

Safe claim: Civic Sentinel authorship of integration, explanation, source-linked benchmark bookmarks, and public-good presentation.

Unsafe claim: exclusive invention of produced-water remote sensing, salinity indices, hydrocarbon indices, heavy-metal indices, vegetation stress mapping, or the PWCI/ASAI/OBEC/LBI/VSI/BPI spectral mechanisms.

## Bookmark Rule

Each composite should maintain at least five dated, sourced scenario bookmarks. These bookmarks are verified place/event/context anchors for inspection and storytelling, not verified Sentinel-2 detections until live imagery and field/source validation are attached.

Every bookmark needs a source label and source URL. Notes should say what the source verifies and should avoid turning a benchmark location into a detection claim.
